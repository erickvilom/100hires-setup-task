## Post 1 (3-part live case study)
**Date:** 2026 (recent)
**Topic:** Live AEO case study — does translating content increase AI search citations?

**Part 1 — Study Setup:**

Brand new AEO / AI Search data study: I've partnered with Weglot to conduct BRAND NEW research into AI search. This is a live case study. I'll share everything in real time.

The hypothesis we're testing: "Does translating a blog post into new languages get your brand cited & recommended more in AI search in those markets?"

How we've set up the study:
1. Chose 15 blog posts from a B2B SaaS client
2. 11 product listicles, 4 educational guides
3. Measured baseline metrics (like clicks & AI citations)
4. Tracked prompts in each language (w. Peec AI)
5. Automatically translated blogs with Weglot
6. We chose two markets: Germany & Spain
7. Indexed the new blogs via Search Console

**Part 2 — Results (30 days):**

This B2B SaaS got 3.83x visibility and +5 demos from translating 14 blog posts.

- +102 new clicks
- +27,797 impressions
- +1,100 AI citations (measured by Bing)
- Spain: 3.83x visibility (6% → 23%)
- Germany: 3.73x visibility (4.5% → 16.8%)
- 2 direct demo requests from new URLs
- +3 uplift in qualified demos in those regions

**Part 3 — Final Results (60 days):**

Month 1 recap:
- +102 clicks, +27,797 impressions

Month 2 results:
